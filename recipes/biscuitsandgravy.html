Biscuits and Gravy

A Southern Breakfast Staple

An absolutely decadent dish that is always gauranteed to warm your soul. This breakfast dish is sure make you slap your momma as my grandfather used to say. In effort to stun your family tomorrow morning gather these ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 4 cups Whole milk
  • 1 lb. Breakfast Sausage
  • 1 pack of Bacon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Biscuits (Store bought will work)

The Process

  1. In two separate pans: Fry up the entire pack of Bacon. Slice half of the sausage and fry. The other half pound of sausage will be crumbled and cooked. The crumbled sausage will be added to the gravy.
  2. Combine sausage and bacon grease in one pan, cook over a low to medium heat.
  3. Slowly start to add in your flour to the grease. Stiring consistenlty as you add it in. The goal is to create a roux, which will be the base of the gravy.
  4. Add flower until you get to the desired thick and creamy consistency. Keep over heat until the color gets to a golden brown.
  5. Add in milk a half cup at a time. Stir Stir Stir Stir. You want the mixture to be as smooth as possible. Once all milk is added and the mixture is the desired consistency begin to season your gravy.
  6. Add in Salt, Pepper, and Red Pepper Flake to taste.
  7. Add in the cooked crumbled sausage and stir.
  8. Bring gravy to a low boil, lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Enjoy and prepare for a late sunday morning nap!
