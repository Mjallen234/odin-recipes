The Best Damn Chili Recipe

Good for the Soul

There is nothing quite like a bowl of the Best Damn Chili on a cool fall evening. Pair with a football game and the combination is simply very hard to beat.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 28oz can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 28oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 28oz can of Chili Beans
  • 1 yellow onion(chopped)
  • 1 Green Bell pepper(diced)
  • 1 Red Bell pepper(diced)
  • Jalapeno Pepper (diced)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Red Pepper Flake
  • 8oz of a Beer of your Choice

How its made

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Chop all of the vegetables and cook down in a pan.
  3. Combine meat, vegetavles, and seasonings in a pot.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 70 minutes.
  5. Garnish with crackers, cheese, onions, and sour cream.
