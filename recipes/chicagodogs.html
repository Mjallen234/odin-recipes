Chicago Style Hot Dogs

A True Chicago Classic

When you think of essential Chicago Foods, it doesnt take long till you get to the one and only Chicago Dog. The best part about this recipe is that its one of the easier ones youll ever make. The fresh ingredients and the "Vienna" snap is what brings this all together.

Ingedients

  • Rosen Hot Dog Buns
  • Vienna Beef Hot Dogs
  • Mustard
  • Relish
  • Sport Peppers
  • White Onion
  • Tomato
  • Kosher Dill Pickle
  • Celery Salt

The Creation

  1. Bring a pot of water to a Boil
  2. Add hot dogs to the water and reduce heat
  3. Steam for 7 minutes
  4. While the hot dogs are cooking, slice your tomato and finely dice onion.
  5. Place hotdog on Poppysead bun
  6. Top with all of your ingredients
  7. Enjoy! Give it a DABEARS when youre done. Welcome to Chicago!
